GPU & Scientific Simulation

My M.S. thesis, ASTRA-GBT, is a multi-GPU framework for acoustic simulation across arbitrary environments.

We pushed it to 2,048 GH200 GPUs on the Shaheen III supercomputer, generating sound profiles in minutes on a city-scale acoustic model .
