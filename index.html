GPU & Scientific Simulation

My M.S. thesis, ASTRA-GBT, is a multi-GPU framework for acoustic simulation across arbitrary environments. We pushed it to 2,048 GH200 GPUs on the Shaheen III supercomputer, achieving 91% strong-scaling efficiency on a city-scale acoustic model.

Teaching & Communication

I've TA'd four courses across my academic career: algorithms, NLP, probability, and numerical linear algebra. Science communication, finding ways to make hard ideas feel accessible, has become one of my main interests outside the lab.
